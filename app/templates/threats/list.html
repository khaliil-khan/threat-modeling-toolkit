STRIDE & PASTA
STRIDE

Spoofing, Tampering, Repudiation, Information Disclosure, Denial of Service, Elevation of Privilege — a threat categorization model.

PASTA

Process for Attack Simulation and Threat Analysis — focuses on Threat Agent, Attack Vector, Vulnerable Asset, Technical Impact, and Business Impact.

DREAD Scoring

DREAD is a risk assessment framework with five factors:

Damage Extent of damage if exploited
Reproducibility Ease of reproducing the attack
Exploitability Difficulty to exploit
Affected Users Scope of potential victims
Discoverability Likelihood of discovery
