STRIDE & PASTA Methodologies
STRIDE

Threat categorization: Spoofing, Tampering, Repudiation, Information Disclosure, Denial of Service, Elevation of Privilege.

PASTA

Attack simulation: Threat Agent, Attack Vector, Vulnerable Asset, Technical Impact, Business Impact.

DREAD Methodology

Risk Assessment Framework: Quantify threats by scoring five key factors (1-5 scale).

D — Damage Potential loss from exploitation
R — Reproducibility Ease of reproducing attack
E — Exploitability Skill/effort required
A — Affected Users Scope of impact
D — Discoverability Likelihood of discovery

Note: DREAD scoring is available for all methodologies to quantify risk.
